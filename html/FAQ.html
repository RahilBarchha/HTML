Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is Student Hub?

Student Hub is an online portal where students can access study materials, timetables, attendance, and important updates.

2. Is Student Hub free to use?

Yes, Student Hub is completely free for students.

3. Can I access the portal on my mobile?

Yes, the portal can be accessed on both mobile phones and computers.

4. How can I contact the Student Hub team?

You can contact us through the Contact Us page using the email or contact form.

5. Can I upload my own notes?

Yes, students can upload notes if this feature is enabled by the administrator.
